Scheduling simplified

Manage patient appointments faster than ever before

  • Book an appointment

    Schedule appointments in a snap with quick patient lookup and built-in address search.

  • Referring doctor

    Easily add referring doctors and customize their preferred report delivery methods for each appointment.

  • Send a copy

    Set additional delivery endpoints to automatically dispatch the report upon radiologist authorization.

  • Billings

    Simplify service item additions using exam codes instead of item numbers, and process payments seamlessly.

  • Preparation

    Send predefined preparation instructions or personalise them before dispatch to cater to individual needs.

  • Attachments

    Minimize paper handling and follow-ups. Attach referrals, worksheets, blood reports, registration forms, and other documents in PDF, DOCX, or JPG formats to the appointment.
