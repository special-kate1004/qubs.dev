Automated data extraction for swift bookings

A distinguishing aspect of our online booking platform is its ability to autonomously extract patient information from uploaded referral documents. This advanced feature significantly minimizes the data entry burden on patients, thereby accelerating the booking process.

With a simple referral document upload, patients can trust that all necessary details will be precisely captured by our system. This not only conserves time but also mitigates the likelihood of manual entry errors. It's an illustration of how we utilize technology to optimize your clinic's operational efficiency.

Confirmation and Preparation Guidance

Upon scheduling an appointment online, patients receive a confirmation SMS encompassing vital details like the appointment's date, time, and location. Additionally, we dispatch tailored ultrasound preparation instructions, ensuring patients are well-informed and prepared for their visit, simplifying the process for both your staff and your clientele.
