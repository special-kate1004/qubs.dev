Manage your organisation

QUBS can consolidate various systems traditionally employed for patient management, Medicare billings, DICOM imaging archives, and image access portals into one. Equip your reception, accounts, operations, and radiologists with the tools necessary to enhance patient care management.

Effortless Medicare management

Streamline your radiology clinic's billing process with QUBS. Our software effortlessly handles batching, submitting, and finalizing claims for you. Plus, stay updated with the latest Medicare pricing schedules, updated automatically as they are released.

Flexible teleradiology

Enable your radiologists to work from any location without the hassle of VPN connections. With QUBS, managing payments is a breeze thanks to automated payouts, simplifying the process of outsourcing reporting to third parties.
