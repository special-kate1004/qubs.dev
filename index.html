Get reports out faster with
our worksheets-to-report AI tool

Scan

Simply select the patient from the DICOM Modality Worklist, start scanning, and choose between using electronic worksheets or attaching handwritten ones. Once ready, send them to the radiologist for reporting.

Report

Initiate your reporting on-site, from home, or on the go, without the need for VPN connections. Dictate directly into QUBS. Choose your preferred method—let typists handle the typing, utilize the built-in voice recognition, or opt for third-party software such as Dragon Medical or M*Modal Fluency.
If you prefer to bring your own DICOM viewer, QUBS fully supports integration with third-party DICOM viewers such as OsiriX, Horos, and InteleViewer.
Explore Reporting

Delivery

Once a report is authorized, QUBS automatically dispatches the final report to the referring doctor, patient, and specialists in their preferred format, including:

  • HL7 via Healthlink or MedicalObjects
  • Fax
  • Email
  • SMS
