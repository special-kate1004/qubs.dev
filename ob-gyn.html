AI-Powered Reporting: Personalized and Efficient

Our advanced system transforms radiology reporting, ensuring reports match your clinic’s style. Using OCR, we capture handwritten and digital worksheets, combine data with ultrasound measurements, and process it through our LLM, trained to mirror your radiologists’ writing. The result: personalized, consistent draft reports that enhance efficiency and patient care.

Secure and Convenient Patient Access to Reports and Images

Give patients secure, convenient access to their ultrasound images and reports with QUBS. After a scan, a unique link is sent for online viewing via our portal. You control report visibility, ensuring access only after the referring doctor’s review. This boosts engagement, streamlines communication, and maintains professional integrity.
